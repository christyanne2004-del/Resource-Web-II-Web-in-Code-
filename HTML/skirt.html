How to attach side skirts to car simple guide



1.Raise the car using a jack or blocks.

The first step is to elevate the car's height so that you can safely work below it because the side skirt trims are fastened beneath the side doors. As you do the installation, make sure the car is securely fastened and won't roll away.

2. Clean the area completely

In order for the adhesive to effectively adhere and produce a firm attachment for the side skirts, it is crucial to carefully clean the area and remove any dirt, debris, or oil. Because it collects mud and debris from the roadways, this section of the car is usually one of the dirtiest, so take your time.

3. Cover each side skirt's edge with mounting tape.

Next, cover the whole perimeter of each side skirt with either Double Sided Mounting Tape or Grey Double Sided Acrylic Tape, leaving the visible border free of tape. This will ensure that your application is safe and impenetrable. Once you've completed wrapping the tape around the outer border that will be attached to the car's underside, firmly press it down using a spreader or your fingers.

4.Take out the release lining

The double-sided tape that is prepared for bonding to the car can now be seen by removing the release liner. Avoid touching the exposed adhesive as this may lessen its efficacy.

5.Attach each side skirt trim under the side doors.

Lastly, use a ruler to equally position each skirt trim to get the proper measurements. Once you're satisfied with the position, firmly press down on the skirt's underside to keep it.
