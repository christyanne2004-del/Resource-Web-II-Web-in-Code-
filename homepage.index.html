A Girl's Guide to Cars

Here is where I Teach Other Women How To Fix Their Cars and much more

How to change a Tire?

As a driver, the best thing to learn is how to change a flat tire. It’s a great idea to be armed with the tools and the know how to change a flat in a pinch.



How to change your oil?

Regular oil changes are super important for keeping your car running smoothly. Knowing how to do it yourself can save you money and keep your engine in top shape



How to change a car battery?

A dead battery can really throw a wrench in your plans. Learning how to swap it out is a handy skill that can get you back on the road fast



How to change brake pads?

Maintaining your brakes is a must for safety. Changing your brake pads is a manageable task, and knowing how to do it can give you peace of mind.



How to change brake fluid?

Brake fluid is often overlooked, but it's critical for your car's braking system. Changing it regularly helps maintain optimal braking performance.
